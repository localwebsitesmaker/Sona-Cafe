About Us

Welcome to LocalBiz, your trusted local business directory. Our mission is to connect communities with the best local businesses in their area.

Founded in 2023, we've helped thousands of customers discover quality services, from restaurants and hotels to healthcare facilities and retail stores.

Our Vision

To become the most reliable platform for discovering and supporting local businesses worldwide.

Our Values

  • Community Focus: We prioritize local businesses and community growth.
  • Accuracy: We verify all business listings to ensure reliable information.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our platform with modern technology.
  • Support: We provide excellent support to both businesses and users.

Developer

This platform was developed by a passionate team of web developers dedicated to creating useful tools for local communities.

Our Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
  • Styling: Custom CSS with CSS Variables for theming
  • Icons: Font Awesome for scalable vector icons
  • Fonts: Google Fonts (Poppins & Montserrat)
  • Images: Unsplash for high-quality, royalty-free images

Features

  • Fully responsive design for all devices
  • Interactive search with real-time filtering
  • Animated transitions and hover effects
  • Modern navigation with hamburger menu
  • Business categorization and detailed views

This project is open-source and available on GitHub. Feel free to contribute!
